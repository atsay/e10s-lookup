Add-on compatibility checker

There are a lot of changes coming up for add-on development. In the next 12-18 months, we will transition to WebExtensions as the standard for creating add-ons. Over the same period of time, existing methods for add-on development such as XUL/XPCOM will be deprecated. Multi-process Firefox (aka Electrolysis, or e10s) is also rolling out, which means some add-on developers will have to update their add-ons more than once.

You can use the tool below to check the compatibility of your add-on as it relates to these changes, and what your options are for transitioning if you are affected. You will need your add-on guid, which is found in the following places:

  • In the <em:id> of install.rdf of your add-on
  • In the id of the package.json for a JPM add-on
  • On addons.mozilla.org, go to Tools > Manage My Submissions > and select the add-on, then scroll down to Technical Details
